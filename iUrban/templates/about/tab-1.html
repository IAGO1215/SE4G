Project Scope

The application of our project is named “iUrban”. By using this application, users can find quantitative or qualitative data descriptions about the urban environment on the website, as well as instructions on how to contribute to the database.

Purpose of developing

Is to inform and involve communities potentially who want to contribute to the project through an online desktop application to access, analyze and visualize the available iUrban data of a given area through an interactive mapping tool

Project Goals

Municipalities could apply it on the urban health or urban economy thesis, for instance, to analyze the relationship between traffic and noise and make city park planning. It could also be conducive for research on urban environments.
